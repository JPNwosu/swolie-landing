Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

Swolie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Our Privacy Commitment

Swolie does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app.

Data That Stays on Your Device

All data you create in Swolie remains exclusively on your device:

  • Workout Data: Your workout logs, dates, and types are stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Progress Photos: Any photos you take are saved to the app's local documents folder on your device.
  • Settings & Preferences: Your app preferences are stored locally.
  • HealthKit Data: If you choose to integrate with Apple Health, that data is managed by Apple and stays in your Health app.

What We Don't Do

Swolie is a completely offline, local-only application. We do not:

  • Collect analytics or usage data
  • Use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs
  • Require user accounts or authentication
  • Make network requests or API calls
  • Share your data with any third parties

Third-Party Services

Swolie uses only Apple's native frameworks. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Security

Since all your data stays on your device, it benefits from your device's built-in security features, including encryption and biometric protection. We never have access to your data because it never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Since Swolie does not collect any personal information from any users, there is no data collection from children. The app can be used by anyone, and all data remains private on the user's device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
